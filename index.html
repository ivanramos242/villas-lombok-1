Why Lombok

An early-stage island destination next to Bali

Lombok is emerging as one of Indonesia’s most compelling property and lifestyle destinations. Located next to Bali but still less saturated, the island offers a rare combination of natural beauty, improving infrastructure, international tourism appeal and a calmer, more authentic atmosphere.

Why Mandalika

Mandalika is becoming South Lombok’s most watched destination

Mandalika sits at the centre of South Lombok’s next phase: international motorsport, coastal tourism, restaurants, beaches, surf culture and infrastructure improvements are bringing new visibility to the area.
